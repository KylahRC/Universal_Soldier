About the company




Business Objectives

Develop a training craft capable of sustained operation in Jupiter's atmosphere

Enhance soldiers' muscular development and overall health through specialized nutrition and training regimens.

Achieve operational efficiency by utilizing hydrogen fuel for propulsion and energy needs.

Establish partnerships with military organizations globally for training programs.


Company Description

Universal Soldier will design and operate a unique training facility suspended within Jupiter's atmosphere. This facility will capitalize on the planet's strong gravitational pull to promote muscular development and physical resilience in soldiers. The craft will support advanced nutritional regimens and provide a sustainable living environment for soldiers.
